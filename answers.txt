Why is it important to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all?\nAnswer: Ensuring access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all is important for several reasons:

1. **Economic Growth**: Access to energy is crucial for economic development. It enables businesses to operate efficiently, creates job opportunities, and supports overall economic growth.

2. **Quality of Life**: Energy is essential for basic needs such as lighting, heating, cooking, and access to clean water. It improves the quality of life for individuals and communities.

3. **Health**: Reliable energy sources are necessary for healthcare facilities to function effectively. Access to energy can improve healthcare services and contribute to better health outcomes.

4. **Education**: Energy is needed for schools to operate, enabling children to study, learn, and access educational resources. It plays a vital role in promoting education and lifelong learning opportunities.

5. **Environmental Sustainability**: Sustainable energy sources help reduce greenhouse gas emissions and mitigate climate change. Access to clean energy contributes to environmental conservation and a healthier planet.

6. **Social Equity**: Ensuring universal access to energy helps reduce inequalities and ensures that everyone, regardless of their socio-economic status, has the opportunity to benefit from energy services.
